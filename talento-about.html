Our story

From the inside out and never looking back.

Talento wasn't born in a boardroom. It started in the thick of it sitting inside organisations, running internal talent functions, learning every discipline from finance and operations to marketing and commercial. That breadth gave us something most specialist recruiters don't have: genuine range, and an understanding of how businesses actually work.

Technology found us or maybe we found it. Either way, it felt like home.

As the tech economy accelerated, so did our focus. GTM, Product, Engineering the disciplines that define whether a tech company scales or stalls. We narrowed in, built deep networks, and developed the kind of market knowledge that only comes from being present for thousands of hiring conversations over years, not months.

Immersive tech was a natural extension spatial computing, XR, the infrastructure of tomorrow. The same problems existed there: misaligned roles, under-qualified briefs, and candidates assessed on proxies rather than real capability. Same problem. Same answer. Better tools.

The beginning
Agency first, then internal — full cycle from day one
We cut our teeth in the agency world, working across multiple disciplines and industries. That sharp commercial edge never left us. We then moved to the internal side — sitting inside organisations, running talent functions, seeing hiring from the inside out. That combination made us something most recruiters aren't: full cycle talent partners who understand both sides of the table completely.

IQS™
Intelligent Qualification System

We'd seen the same failure repeat across hundreds of engagements: roles that were poorly scoped, candidates who were poorly understood, and matches made on gut feel and speed. Friction everywhere. Outcomes that disappointed everyone.

So we built IQS™ not as a product, but as the answer to a problem we knew intimately. A system that qualifies both sides before a single meeting is booked. Role intelligence. Candidate intelligence. True matching.

It's our proudest achievement not because it's clever technology, but because it genuinely works. It removes friction. It finds the real match. And it's built into everything we do.
